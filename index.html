Hello, I am Juliette

Sport has shaped my life from an early age. I grew up reading L’Équipe and watching the Tour de France and the Olympic Games, and today sport continues to influence the way I think, work and tell stories. For me, it is more than a passion - it is a framework that teaches resilience, discipline and adaptability. What drives me most is telling athlete stories that move people - stories that inspire and create emotion. I believe powerful narratives can elevate a brand and give meaning to major sporting moments, whether through athletes, product launches or international competitions. I am also deeply committed to increasing the visibility of women in sport. Representation matters, and I aim to amplify the voices and achievements of female athletes, ensuring their strength and excellence are fully recognized.
